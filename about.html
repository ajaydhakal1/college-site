About Us

Tri Shaheed Model School Since 1987

In the era of Rana, education in Nepal was not developed. In 1987, a resident of Panchamool in Syangja District Andhikhola Rural Municipality Ward No. 1, Subedar Major Gambhir Singh Gurung, established a language school in response to the need for formal education in the area. The school has evolved over time to meet the changing needs of its students. In 2063, it was transformed into a high school, and in 2070, with permission from the Government of Nepal, it added computer engineering subjects at the secondary level (grades 9-12). Known as Tri-Shaheed Ma.Vi, the school has become famous in the Gandaki province, and operates from nursery to class 12, both in the general stream and in the technical stream at the secondary level
